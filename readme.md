# Retail Sales Performance Analysis & Business Insights

## Problem Statement
Analyze sales data to identify key factors affecting revenue and profitability and provide actionable business recommendations.

## Tools Used
- Python
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Seaborn
- Matplotlib

## Key Insights
- High sales do not always lead to high profit
- Excessive discounting leads to profit loss
- Furniture category is underperforming
- Technology is the most profitable category

## Key Recommendations
- Optimize discount strategy
- Improve Furniture pricing
- Focus on high-profit categories

## Conclusion
The primary issue affecting profitability is excessive discounting, especially in the Furniture category.
