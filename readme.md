# 📊 Retail Sales Performance Analysis & Profit Optimization

## 🔍 Problem Statement
Analyze retail sales data to identify key drivers of revenue and profitability, and provide actionable insights to improve business performance.


## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- SQL (data extraction & aggregation)
- Python (Pandas, NumPy)
- Data Visualization (Seaborn, Matplotlib)


## 📊 Key Business Insights

- The business generated ~$2.3M in total sales with ~$292K profit, indicating overall profitability with scope for improvement  
- The Technology category delivers the highest profit, while Furniture shows low profitability despite high sales  
- Tables and Bookcases are the most loss-making sub-categories, with Tables contributing the highest losses  
- Discounts above 20% lead to negative profit, making excessive discounting the primary driver of losses  
- The West region performs best in terms of both sales and profit, while the South region underperforms  


## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Optimize discount strategy by limiting discounts above 20%  
- Re-evaluate pricing and cost structure in the Furniture category  
- Reduce losses in sub-categories like Tables and Bookcases  
- Focus on expanding high-performing regions such as the West  
- Improve performance in underperforming regions like the South  


## 📌 Conclusion

The analysis reveals that excessive discounting is the primary factor affecting profitability, particularly in the Furniture category. By optimizing pricing strategies and focusing on high-performing segments, the business can significantly improve overall profitability.
